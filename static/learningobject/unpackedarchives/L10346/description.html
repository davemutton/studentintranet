Title: ESL Readers: A mysterious meeting 
Description: Read and listen to a story of a boy who responds to a series of mysterious commands to go to different places and wear various disguises. Share his confusion until the end, when a surprise is revealed. Use the glossary tool if you are unsure of a word. Then try some follow-up activities to test your understanding of the vocabulary and sentence structures used in the story. 
Topics: Comics; Disguise; English (Second language); Language; Questions; Vocabulary 
Keywords: Comics, Disguise, English (Second language), Grammatical tense, Language, Problem sets, Questions, Vocabulary 
Learning area: English 
Strand: Listening and speaking; Reading and viewing 
Concept:  
Audience user level: 5; 6; 7; 8; 9; 10  Audience type: Student 
Age range: 9-16  Audience sector: School 
Key learning objectives:
  • Students use contextual clues to deduce meaning from a story.
  • Students identify the purpose of, and participants in, a text.
  • Students practise the key vocabulary and sentence structures used in each story.
Educational value:
  • Presents the story as a comic, with strong visual support.
  • Supports development of students' reading and listening skills by providing the story in both written and audio formats.
  • Provides a comprehensive glossary to support each page of the comic.
  • Includes a wonderword activity in which students find words in English.
  • Provides a matching activity in which students flip cards to find pairs (with matching options including audio to text, audio to picture, and text to picture).
  • Includes a make-a-picture activity in which students consider a clue (audio, text or graphic) and then find its matching audio, text or graphic item.
  • Includes a quiz activity that reinforces key vocabulary from the story.
  • Includes a story-sequencing activity in which students match texts to pictures.
Student activity: Interactives; Modelling
