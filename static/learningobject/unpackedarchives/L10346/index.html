Interactive stories and games for
ESL beginners


ESL Readers is a series of interactive literacy resources for students who are in the early stages of learning English as a second language. There are three animated stories:
  • A mysterious meeting
  • Sick at school
  • Mandy's bad day
The stories cover common themes such as school, illness, food, transport, clothing, colour and shops and services. Owing to the themes explored and the humorous treatment of the stories, these resources are suitable for a broad range of students in primary and secondary schools.
All three titles have a separate teachers' resource, which is available in 22 different languages.
