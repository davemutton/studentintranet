Sugoi! Let's eat: eating and drinking

Acknowledgements

Sugoi! was first published by Curriculum Corporation in association with The New South Wales Department of Education and Training. © Commonwealth of Australia 2000

Description

Explore the Japanese names for a variety of foods and drinks. Listen to the conversation between a chef and the people making an order. Match each order to the correct food or drink icon. Score points for each correct answer.

Instructions

  1. Click on the turtle to hear instructions.
  2. Click on a food or drink icon to order something to eat or drink.
  3. Click on the chef to have it served.
  4. Click on the boy or girl to repeat the food or drink order.
  5. Click and hold the Hiragana button to see the Japanese text.
  6. Click and hold the English button to see the translation.
  7. Click on the cube to play the game.
  8. Click on the turtle to repeat the game instructions.
  9. To play the game, listen to each order then click on the matching food or drink icon.
  10. Click on the smile to repeat a phrase.
  11. For each correct answer you will get 5 points. For each incorrect answer you lose 5 points from your bonus.
  12. Click on the cube to return to the introduction.
  13. Click on the arrow to restart the game.

Modules

  1. Sugoi! Let's eat: eating and drinking
  2. Sugoi! Let's eat: the fruit shop
  3. Sugoi! Let's eat: what's cooking?

Sugoi! Let's eat: the fruit shop

Acknowledgements

Sugoi! was first published by Curriculum Corporation in association with The New South Wales Department of Education and Training. © Commonwealth of Australia 2000

Description

Explore the Japanese names for fruits at the fruit shop. Listen to the conversations between Tomoko and the shopkeeper. Use a boy's digital shopping list to select fruit and whether it is or is not available in the fruit shop. Score points for each correct answer.

Instructions

  1. Click on the turtle to hear instructions.
  2. Click on a fruit icon to hear the Berti's question.
  3. Click on the shopkeeper to hear her answer.
  4. Click on Berti to repeat the question.
  5. Click and hold the Hiragana button to see the Japanese text.
  6. Click and hold the English button to see the translation.
  7. Click on the cube to play the game.
  8. Click on the turtle to repeat the game instructions.
  9. To play the game, listen to the conversation between Berti and the shopkeeper to find out what food is available.
  10. Click on the pink arrows to select the correct fruit on Berti's digital shopping list.
  11. Click on the tick if the fruit is in the store and click the cross if it is not in the store.
  12. Click on the smile to repeat a phrase.
  13. For each correct answer you will get 5 points. For each incorrect answer you lose 5 points from your bonus.
  14. Click on the cube to return to the introduction.
  15. Click on the arrow to restart the game.

Sugoi! Let's eat: what's cooking?

Acknowledgements

Sugoi! was first published by Curriculum Corporation in association with The New South Wales Department of Education and Training. © Commonwealth of Australia 2000

Description

Listen to the sequence of phrases for preparing, cooking and eating food. Match the correct sequence numbers and phrases to each stage of the cooking session. Score points for each correct answer.

Instructions

  1. Click on the turtle to hear instructions.
  2. Click on the cards in the correct order to hear the phrases for preparing, cooking and eating a meal.
  3. Click and hold the Hiragana button to see the Japanese text.
  4. Click and hold the English button to see the translation.
  5. Click on the cube to play the game.
  6. Click on the turtle to repeat the game instructions.
  7. To play the game, click and drag the speech bubbles and numbers to the correct pictures in the sequence.
  8. Roll over the speech bubbles and numbers to hear each phrase.
  9. Listen to the complete sequence after you have correctly moved each item.
  10. For each correct answer you will get 5 points. For each incorrect answer you lose 5 points from your bonus.
  11. Click on the cube to return to the introduction.
  12. Click on the arrow to restart the game.

  1. Sugoi! Let's eat: eating and drinking
  2. Sugoi! Let's eat: the fruit shop
  3. Sugoi! Let's eat: what's cooking?
