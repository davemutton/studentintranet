Title: Platonic solids 
Description: Investigate the five Platonic solids by displaying, rotating and resizing them. Select vertices, edges, and faces to show that the number of vertices minus the number of edges plus the number of faces is equal to 2 (Euler’s formula). 
Topics: Nets (Geometry); Solids (Geometry); Translation (Geometry) 
Keywords: Nets (Geometry), Solids (Geometry), Translation (Geometry) 
Learning area: Mathematics 
Strand: Measurement and geometry 
Concept: Estimation; Graphs; Linear equations; Modelling 
Audience user level: 3; 4; 7  Audience type: Student 
Age range: 7-13  Audience sector: School 
Key learning objectives:
  • Students investigate the five Platonic solids.
  • Students demonstrate Euler’s formula by applying it to the Platonic solids.
Educational value:
  • Allows rotation and resizing of Platonic solids.
  • Supports repeated use through random generation of calculations of Euler's formula.
  • Allows students to manipulate 3-dimensional shapes to investigate a formula.
