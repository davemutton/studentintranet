Title: Letter planet: igh, ear, str 
Description: Help a stranded space traveller return home by filling three fuel tanks with words that have the same letter pattern. Select words with combinations of 'igh', 'ear' or 'str'. Read and listen to model words. Select similar words with the same pattern and place them in the fuel tank. Then construct sentences by putting words in the appropriate order. Identify the word with the chosen letter combinations. This learning object is one of a series of five objects.

Key learning objectives:
  • Students identify words containing the three-letter clusters: igh, ear and str.
  • Students construct sentences by placing words in the correct order.
Educational value:
  • Models how the sound and three-letter cluster 'igh' as in 'night' forms part of a word.
  • Models how the sound and three-letter cluster 'ear' as in 'clear' forms part of a word.
  • Models how the sound and three-letter cluster 'str' as in 'strange' forms part of a word.
  • Includes audio representation of written words containing the three-letter clusters: igh, ear and str.
  • Provides opportunities to identify words containing three-letter clusters: igh, ear and str.
  • Highlights importance of word order in the construction of sentence meaning.
  • Structured feedback includes audio support of three-letter cluster sounds.
